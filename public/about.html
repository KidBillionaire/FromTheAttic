The Story

My passion for vintage clothing started when I discovered the unique stories behind each piece. Every day, I hunt through LA's hidden corners - from dusty thrift stores to estate sales - searching for those one-of-a-kind treasures that deserve a second life.

What makes From the Attic special is our dedication to finding not just vintage clothes, but pieces that tell a story. Each item in our collection is personally selected by me, ensuring that you're getting something truly special.

Whether you're looking for a perfectly worn-in pair of Levi's or a rare band tee, I'm here to help you find exactly what you're looking for. If you have something specific in mind, don't hesitate to reach out - I love the thrill of the hunt!
